XCSoar is a tactical glide computer for glider pilots. Free and open-source. No ads. No tracking. Runs on Android, iOS, Linux, macOS, and Windows.

FEATURES

• Moving map with terrain, airspace, airports, turnpoints, and task
• Infoboxes: altitudes, thermals, MacCready, navigation, task, weather, safety
• FLARM traffic and glide computer
• Task declaration and support for many vario/NMEA devices
• Barogram, polar, thermal analysis, OLC optimization
• Waypoint, airspace, and map data: CUP, OpenAir, XCM, and more

WHY XCSoar?

• No ads, no tracking, no data collection
• Works offline; your flight data stays on your device
• Multi-platform: phone, tablet, PC, Kobo
• Open-source alternative to commercial glide computers
• Supports a wide range of hardware (FLARM, LX, Vega, and many others)

Developed with love by the open-source community. Manual and more: https://xcsoar.org
