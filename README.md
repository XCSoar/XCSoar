**XCSoar** is a tactical glide computer for glider pilots. Free and open-source. No ads. No tracking. Runs on Android, iOS, Linux, macOS, and Windows. Developed with love by the [open-source community](https://xcsoar.org/develop/).

## Features

XCSoar includes the tools most glider pilots need for cross-country flying:

- Moving map with terrain, airspace, airports, and task navigation
- Context-sensitive infoboxes with altitude, navigation, glide
  calculations, wind, weather, and task data
- Traffic display and glide computer calculations
- Task planning/declaration with support for many vario and logger
  devices
- Flight analysis tools such as barograph, polar view, and OLC
  optimization
- Thermal centering and support for online thermal sources
- Import support for common waypoint, airspace, and map formats
  (including CUP, CUPX, OpenAir, and XCM)

## Why XCSoar?

XCSoar is built for pilots, by pilots:

- No ads, no tracking, no data collection, and no account required
- Works offline; your flight data stays on your device
- Multi-platform: phone, tablet, PC, Kobo, and single-board/embedded
  computers (Raspberry Pi, [OpenVario](https://openvario.org))
- Open-source alternative to commercial glide computers
- Supports a wide range of hardware
- Supported device drivers include:
  - Collision avoidance: [FLARM](https://flarm.com/),
    [Stratux](https://stratux.me/)
  - Varios: [XCVario](https://xcvario.com/),
    [CAI 302](https://cambridge-aero.com/300series.htm),
    [OpenVario](https://openvario.org),
    [Borgelt B50](https://www.borgeltinstruments.com/?page_id=182),
    [Flymaster](https://www.flymaster.net/),
    [Flytec](https://www.flytec.com/),
    [XCTracer](https://www.xctracer.com/en),
    [LXNAV Vario S* and V7 series](https://www.lxnav.com/),
    [LXNAVIGATION ERA variometer](https://www.lxnavigation.com/),
    [Loefgren vario](https://www.lofgren-electronics.fr/)
  - Radios: [AIR Avionics ACD-57](https://www.air-avionics.com/comm-and-transponders/),
    [TQ-Aviation (KRT2)](https://www.tq-aviation.com/),
    [XCOM 760](https://www.mcp.com.au/xcom760/),
    [ATR833](https://www.funkeavionics.de/en/produkt/atr833s/)
  - Simulator: [Condor 3 flight simulator](https://www.condorsoaring.com/)
