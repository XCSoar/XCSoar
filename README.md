**XCSoar** is a tactical glide computer for glider pilots. Free and open-source. No ads. No tracking. Runs on Android, iOS, Linux, macOS, and Windows. Developed with love by the [open-source community](https://xcsoar.org/develop/).

[![F-Droid](doc/badges/f-droid.png)](https://f-droid.org/packages/org.xcsoar/) [![Google Play](doc/badges/google-play.png)](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.xcsoar.play) [![App Store](doc/badges/app-store.png)](https://apps.apple.com/app/xcsoar/id6741046464)

## Features

XCSoar is the ultimate companion for cross-country and competition pilots:

- Moving map with terrain, airspace, airports, turnpoints, and task
- Infoboxes: altitudes, thermals, MacCready, navigation, task, weather, safety
- FLARM traffic and glide computer
- Task declaration and support for many vario/NMEA devices
- Barogram, polar, thermal analysis, OLC optimization
- Waypoint, airspace, and map data: CUP, OpenAir, XCM, and more
- Free and open-source

## Why XCSoar?

XCSoar is built for pilots, by pilots:

- No ads, no tracking, no data collection
- Works offline; your flight data stays on your device
- Multi-platform: phone, tablet, PC, Kobo
- Open-source alternative to commercial glide computers
- Supports a wide range of hardware (FLARM, LX, Vega, and many others)
